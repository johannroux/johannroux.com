I’m a software engineering manager and backend engineer from Lyon, currently based in Berlin.

Over the years I’ve worked across **health tech**, **e-commerce**, and **venture building**—often in environments where reliability and clarity matter as much as speed.

## What I optimize for

- **Confidence**: predictable delivery, transparent trade-offs, and systems that behave under pressure.
- **Craft**: strong defaults, observability, and clean interfaces that make change safe.
- **People**: alignment, ownership, and a culture where feedback is frequent and kind.

## Working style

I like crisp goals, small feedback loops, and written decision records. I’m at my best when I can partner with product and design early, and when the engineering team has the space to build quality in—not bolt it on later.
