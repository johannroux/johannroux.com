The best teams don’t just ship—they ship **predictably**.

Confidence comes from boring (and powerful) habits:

- Clear ownership and decision-making.
- Small changes, frequent releases.
- Observability by default (logs, metrics, traces).
- A culture where “I don’t know yet” is safe to say.

When trust is high, velocity follows.
